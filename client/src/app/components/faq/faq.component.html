Frequently Asked Questions

What is Quitzz?

Quitzz is a project made by students whose goal is to help people break their addiction and feel less alone in this journey. The idea is to build a tool where we can all see each other's progress and realize the extent of this addiction with concrete figures.

What is PornFree?

Being porn free simply means abstaining from watching pornography and more generally from seeking sexual arousal from any medium (video, image, audio...).

Keep in mind that there is no restriction concerning real sex (i.e., with a human partner) as the goal of quitting porn is to be able to live your best life, and we believe that a sexual life with high libido is an important part of it.

What is PMO?

PMO stands for Pornography, Masturbation, Orgasm. Therefore, having a PMO free lifestyle means having a PornFree lifestyle in addition to abstaining from any form of masturbation.

Keep in mind that there is no restriction concerning real sex (i.e., with a human partner) as the goal of quitting PMO is to be able to enjoy a good life while being the best version of yourself, and we believe that a sexual life with high libido is an important part of it.

How is this project funded?

We are relying on free credits and student offers that are available on some hosting services. However, we're not going to have free credits forever! We really don't want to put adds in there.. Any donations is truly helpful.

How does it work?

The objective is to maintain the longest possible streak. In the ranking corresponding to your goal ( pornfree or PMO ) you can press the Commit button which increases your counter. You can only press the button once per day (before midnight).

What if I forget to commit?

Next time you commit, you will actually commit not only that day, but all the days you have skipped automatically. The number on the Commit button indicates the number of days that will be committed.

What if I relapse?

You can press the Relapse button in order to reset your counter.

What about cheating?

We have no ways to know whether someone is cheating or not, and we do not care. If having a high streak without deserving it makes some people happy, then this project might not be of much help to them.

Keep in mind that being honest with ourselves is key in order to overcome this addiction. Therefore, those who cheat will likely be trapped in an infinite loop of relapse.

Who are you?

We are two students struggling with the same addiction problem. Things started to improve dramatically once we discovered the power of not being alone in this journey. Since we spent a good amount of time on online communities, we were very aware of the extent of this problem. Therefore, we decided to create a tool that we think could make people feel less lonely in this battle. In addition to helping people, we want to make people more aware of the dangers of this addiction. We find the fact that our society has completely trivialized pornography very disturbing.

What time zone is used?

We use the time zone information that is provided by your browser. It might not be accurate. If you think that we have the wrong time zone, or simply would like to change your time zone, you can do so in your account page.
